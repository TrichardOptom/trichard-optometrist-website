Our Services

Professional Eye Care Tailored to You

At Trichard Optometrists, we combine decades of experience with modern technology to provide comprehensive eye care for the whole family. Our dedicated team is committed to protecting your vision and helping you find the perfect eyewear to suit your lifestyle.

Our Professional Services

From routine eye examinations to specialised contact lenses and premium eyewear, we offer a complete range of optometric services under one roof.

Comprehensive Eye Examinations

Thorough eye examinations using advanced diagnostic technology to assess your vision and overall eye health.

Contact Lens Clinic

Professional fitting and aftercare for soft, rigid gas permeable (RGP), scleral, coloured and cosmetic contact lenses.

Prescription Lenses

Single vision, bifocal, progressive and occupational lenses with premium coatings and customised solutions.

Frames & Eyewear

A wide range of quality frames, designer collections, sports eyewear, children's frames and sunglasses.

Children's Eye Care

Comprehensive eye care designed to support children's vision, learning and development.

Diagnosis & Treatment

Professional diagnosis, treatment and referral for a variety of eye conditions using modern clinical techniques.

Medical Aid Assistance

Friendly guidance with medical aid benefits, claims, quotations and affordable cash package options.

One-Day Service

Thanks to our trusted local laboratories, we are able to offer efficient one-day service when required.
