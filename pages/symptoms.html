Common Eye Symptoms

Your Eyes Tell an Important Story

Changes in your vision or eye comfort should never be ignored. Learn about common symptoms below and when it may be time to schedule a comprehensive eye examination.

Common Symptoms We Assess

Click on any symptom to learn more.

Dry Eyes

Burning, irritation, redness or watery eyes caused by poor tear quality or quantity.

Blurred Vision

Difficulty seeing clearly at any distance, which may indicate a prescription change or another eye condition.

Headaches

Frequent headaches can sometimes be associated with eye strain or uncorrected vision problems.

Digital Eye Strain

Eye fatigue, dry eyes and headaches caused by prolonged computer, tablet or phone use.

Light Sensitivity

Discomfort when exposed to bright light, sunlight or headlights.

Double Vision

Seeing two images instead of one should always be assessed promptly by an eye care professional.

Floaters & Flashes

New floaters or flashes of light may require urgent assessment.

Eye Pain or Redness

Painful or persistently red eyes should always be professionally evaluated.

Concerned About Your Vision?

If you are experiencing any of these symptoms, our experienced team is here to help. Early detection can make a significant difference to your eye health.
