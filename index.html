Our Professional Services

At Trichard Optometrists, we believe exceptional vision begins with exceptional care. Since 1939, we have proudly provided comprehensive optometric services using advanced technology, clinical expertise and personalised treatment plans for every patient.

👁️ Comprehensive Eye Examinations

Every eye examination includes a thorough assessment of your vision and eye health. We evaluate visual performance, screen for eye diseases and provide tailored recommendations to help protect your eyesight for years to come.

👓 Premium Frames & Eyewear

Discover an extensive collection of quality frames designed for every lifestyle. Whether you require everyday spectacles, sports eyewear, safety eyewear or specialist frames, our experienced team will help you find the perfect fit.

🔍 Contact Lens Consultations

We provide professional fittings and aftercare for a wide range of contact lenses, including Soft Contact Lenses, Rigid Gas Permeable (RGP) Lenses, Scleral Lenses, Coloured Contact Lenses and Cosmetic Contact Lenses.

✨ Premium Spectacle Lenses

We offer high-quality spectacle lenses together with premium coatings including anti-reflective coatings, blue light protection, UV protection, scratch-resistant coatings, photochromic lenses and polarised lenses to suit your individual lifestyle.

👶 Children's Eye Care

Healthy vision plays a vital role in every child's learning and development. Our children's eye examinations are designed to detect vision problems early and support lifelong healthy eyesight.

❤️ Eye Health Management & Professional Referrals

We diagnose, monitor and manage a wide range of eye conditions. Where specialised treatment is required, we work closely with trusted ophthalmologists and other healthcare professionals to ensure our patients receive seamless ongoing care.
